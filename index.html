What we do

Our group focuses on population, community and landscape ecology (sometimes blended with other disciplines). We use modelling tools – both statistics and theoretical models – to study a broad range of ecosystems, aquatic and terrestrial. Most of us are/were affiliated with the University of Bordeaux – previously within the frame of the Labex COTE. The ITE group is co-headed by Laure Carassou (INRAE) and Frederic Barraquand (CNRS).
