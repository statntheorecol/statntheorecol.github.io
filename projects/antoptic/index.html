ANTOPTIC

The ANTOPTIC (Analysis of trophic network to optimize the delivery of natural pest control services) project, led by SAVE and BioGeCo (INRA), aims to answer main two questions:

1) What are the effects of farming practices and landscape context on the structure of arthropod food webs in vineyards?

2) What is the relationship between the structure of arthopod food webs and the levels of pest control ?

The project combines an experimental and a modeling approach. Based on an experimental design composed by 42 vineyards, arthopod predator diets have been analysed using a metabarcoding approach. This information on predator diet is then used to predict the level of pest control services in vineyards across landscapes.
