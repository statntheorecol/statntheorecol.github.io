Our group focuses on population, community and landscape ecology (sometimes blended with other disciplines). We use modelling tools - both statistics and theoretical models - to study ecosystems ranging from forests to the coastal ocean. Most of us are/were affiliated with the University of Bordeaux, within the frame of the Labex COTE, a multi-lab initiative to foster ecological research across ecosystems and disciplines. The ITE group is co-headed by Laure Carassou (INRAE) and Frederic Barraquand (CNRS).
