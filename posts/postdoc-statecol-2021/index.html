Postdoctoral position in statistical ecology

I have a new postdoc position opening (details in linked pdf) on improving dynamic models including interactions within and between species, notably their identifiability properties, by combining different kinds of data.

I would be open to a variety of candidate profiles, ranging from somebody with statistical or probability background that would focus on mathematical aspects of identifiability, to somebody more interested in fitting dynamic models to data. Don’t hesitate to contact me for an informal chat if you may be interested. – Frederic
