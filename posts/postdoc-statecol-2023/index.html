Postdoctoral position(s)

—Now filled—

A new postdoc position is available for 1.5 years. Funding comes from a French Research Agency grant dealing with statistical identifiability, dynamic models including interactions within and between species, and integrated population modelling. If any of those themes interest you, don’t hesitate to contact me.

I would be open to a variety of candidate profiles, ranging from somebody with statistical or probability background that would focus on mathematical aspects of identifiability, to somebody more interested in fitting dynamic models to real or simulated data. The position should start early 2024. Another postdoc on data integration for biodiversity monitoring will also start around that time, in collaboration with Biogeco and other INRAE/OFB colleagues.

A position on the statistical analysis of urban biodiversity data should also become available at some point.

– Frederic
